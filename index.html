About Me

Hello, I'm Adam. I graduated from Bradley University with a BS in Computer Science. I worked in Information Technology and software development throughout my professional career. The majority of my work experience has involved working with data (Oracle PL/SQL & SSMS). I have also worked with Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, and Visual Basic. I enjoy working with technology because it forces me to keep learning new things.

For the past 2.5 years, I have been working at Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University as a systems analyst. My main roles are supporting the admission CRM software (Slate) and ensuring others in the organization can access the data they need in the underlying database. I have also been training the rest of my team in SQL. Prior to working at Kellogg, I worked at Applications Research Corporation for 12 years to help develop and support their CRM software.

On a personal level, I've always lived in Illinois. My hobbies are video gaming and board gaming. My favorite activities are hiking, biking, and eating. My interests include movies, TV, and - most importantly - my wife.
